## Key Decisions Made

| Decision | Choice | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Primary comparison item | spring-petclinic (not gs-rest-service) | Simple items hit 100% coverage ceiling — no signal for variant differentiation |
| N=3 sweep scope | All 7 variants (not just top 4) | Publishable Markov P-matrix fingerprint test requires all variants |
| Markov validity threshold | LR p>0.05, KL<0.10 bits, K-fold MAE<15 steps | From N=1 baseline: KL=0.457 bits (sparse), LR p=0.11 (underpowered) — need N=3 |
| Stage 4 priority ordering | hardened+sae, hardened, forge, hardened+skills+sae | SAE 70-step result + forge quality consistency are the two unconfirmed claims |
| Forge explore prompt fix | Replaced `knowledge/index.md` read with `spring-testing-router` skill | Without KB, reading `knowledge/index.md` returns isError=true and degraded T3 from 0.933 → 0.78 |
